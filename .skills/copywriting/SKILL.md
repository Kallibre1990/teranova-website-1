# Teranova — Copywriting

Premium and to the point. Confident, no "water", no marketing pathos. **Trust is
built on facts and process**, not adjectives. Approved RU reference copy is in
`docs/COPY.md`; the binding rules are in `docs/CONTENT-RULES.md`.

## Voice
- Short, declarative sentences. Concrete verbs: находим, проверяем, сопровождаем.
- Superheadings use parentheses: `( Корея → мир )`, `( Доверие )`, `( Под заказ )`.
- Headlines 700–800 weight; the hero headline uses the metallic silver gradient.

## Hard rules (do not break — see CONTENT-RULES)
1. The word **“guarantee” / “гарантия”** is forbidden (and its equivalents in any
   language). We **“coordinate and support”** (координируем и сопровождаем).
2. Two verification statuses, fixed wording, documented fact (not a promise):
   «Проверено — личный визит» / «Данные предоставлены компанией».
3. **RU = Russian only.** No English words — especially category names
   («Косметика и уход (OEM/ODM)», not “K-beauty”; «Судовое снабжение», not “marine”).
   EN = English, KO = Korean. Never mix.
4. **Never mention “Busan”.** Just “Korea”. Presence on the ground: Korea · Japan ·
   China · Türkiye; other regions on request. Buyer markets: CIS · Europe · Türkiye · USA · Asia.
5. Transport: commercial / passenger / special vehicles + parts = yes. Consumer
   cars = never mention as a direction.
6. **Honesty:** invent no numbers (no “100+ clients”, “15 years”, “35 awards”),
   no fake company names, prices, laws, or certificates.
7. No sanctions / export-control / compliance topics on the public site.
8. Demand-first: always keep the “On demand / Под заказ” block with a working form.
